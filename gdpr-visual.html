💡
Bottom line: In our prototype, GDPR is a sticky note — a label you put on a policy to say "this is why this policy exists." In Collibra, GDPR is a living object — it has its own page, its own articles, it knows which tables and policies are linked to it, it fires workflows, and it shows you a live compliance dashboard. Both start from the same idea: link a policy to a regulation. Collibra just built 5 years of product depth on top of that. Our foundation is correct — it just needs those layers added over time.
