# Why data governance matters now

- **AI raises the stakes** — AI is only as trustworthy as the data beneath it; governance is now a prerequisite.
- **Data outgrew control** — thousands of tables across clouds and warehouses, with unclear ownership.
- **Regulation keeps expanding** — GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA and a growing patchwork of rules.
- **Ungoverned data is costly** — duplicated work, slow access, failed audits, avoidable risk.

---

# The reality of ungoverned data

Across most organizations, governance is run on tribal knowledge, spreadsheets, and email — not a system.

- **Scattered** — data spread across many clouds and storage, each with its own access model
- **Unowned** — no clear owner or steward; definitions conflict between departments
- **Opaque** — no one can answer what data exists, whether it's trustworthy, or who can use it
- **Manual** — policy and approvals enforced by hand: slow, inconsistent, hard to audit

---

# What breaks without governance

- **Hard to find & trust** — fragmented search and duplicate data → wasted effort, bad decisions
- **Sensitive data exposed** — no classification → PII and regulated data sit unprotected
- **Compliance & breach risk** — inconsistent policy and access → exposure and audit failures
- **No lineage or evidence** — can't prove provenance → slow, painful, costly audits

---

# Today's tools force a trade-off

**Heavyweight legacy suites** — powerful and complete… but slow to deploy, costly, and need a large stewardship team.

**Lightweight catalogs** — fast and developer-friendly… but thin on access control and weak on compliance.

**Solix EDG closes the gap** — a modern, fast experience plus enterprise-grade, domain-scoped access control and built-in compliance.

# The governance lifecycle

**Discover → Classify → Govern → Monitor → Improve → Audit**

A continuous loop: each cycle of discovery and improvement is enforced by policy and recorded for audit.

---

# Discover: Data Catalog, metadata & lineage

Bring your data estate into one searchable, continuously refreshed catalog — with full lineage.

- **Unified data catalog** — databases, schemas, tables/views and storage containers in one place
- **Asset profiles & certification** — rich profiles with certification signals that make trust visible
- **Global search** — search across assets and metadata to find the right data fast
- **Asset & column lineage** — trace data flow for impact analysis, root cause, and provenance

---

# Understand: Glossary, tags & classifications

Layer shared business meaning and sensitivity labels onto technical metadata.

- **Business glossary** — controlled vocabulary and definitions, curated with steward approvals
- **Tags & classifications** — apply sensitivity labels and tags consistently across assets
- **Drives downstream control** — classification feeds policy enforcement and compliance
- **Steward-curated** — trust is curated by domain stewards, not left to chance

---

# Trust: Data quality you can measure

Make trust evidence-based with pervasive testing, scoring, and incident workflows.

- **Data-quality tests** — completeness, validity, freshness and more
- **Quality scores** — visible per-asset scores so consumers know what to rely on
- **Incident workflows** — surface, route, and resolve quality issues with alerts
- **Data contracts** — producer/consumer expectations for schema and freshness

---

# Govern: Domains, roles & policy-based access

*Our strongest area.* Enterprise-grade, domain-scoped RBAC with reversible, auditable policy management.

- **Four roles + custom** — Admin (system), Connection Admin, Steward, Viewer — plus custom roles
- **Policy Manager** — policies → rules → conditions, each backed by an editable policy
- **Domain-scoped permissions** — least-privilege access bounded to a steward's assigned domains
- **Domains & data products** — team-based ownership of domains and curated data products

---

# Operate: Stewardship inbox & workflows

Turn governance intent into accountable action with inboxes and approval workflows.

- **Stewardship inbox** — team work queues so governance tasks never fall through the cracks
- **Approval workflows** — multi-step approval and certification flows with notifications
- **Access requests** — request → approve, with the decision written to the audit log
- **Certification** — stewards certify assets as trusted, surfaced at the point of use

---

# Comply: Regulation linking & audit

Connect regulations to classified data and prove it — continuously, not at deadline.

- **Regulation linking** — link GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOC 2 to classified data, owners, and policy
- **Immutable audit logs** — every action attributable and recorded for defensible evidence
- **Compliance posture** — dashboards and notifications track posture across the estate
- **Evidence on demand** — answer auditors in minutes, not weeks

# How it works — live demo

1. **Connect** — connect a source; metadata auto-ingested into the catalog
2. **Curate** — steward adds glossary terms, tags, classification & certification
3. **Test** — quality tests run; scores and incidents surface
4. **Govern** — Policy Manager enforces domain-scoped, role-based access
5. **Approve** — approval workflow routes the request; written to audit log
6. **Comply** — compliance view links regulations to classified assets
